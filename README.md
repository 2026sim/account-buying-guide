# Account Buying Guide

## English

Account Buying Guide provides a comprehensive overview of digital account marketplaces, including social media accounts, email accounts, and various online service accounts.

Digital accounts are widely used in marketing, business operations, and online communication. Understanding different types of accounts such as Gmail, Facebook, Telegram, and TikTok is essential for efficient usage.

This guide helps users learn about account categories, basic features, and common usage scenarios.
